MyROUTE

We believe you deserve an easier life- that is why we are giving you MyROUTE.

This is a platform created to help and assist people/citizens from different places within Lagos in giving direction to their routes with ease using BRT bus.

MyROUTE will let you know the nearest BRT bus-stop to get you to your destination. Also you are able to know when the next available bus will arrive at a bustop or if there are buses in another terminal close to you.
